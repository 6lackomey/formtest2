About us

Welcome to Gifted-hands Landscapers, a premier landscaping company offering high-quality services to clients in Dublin, Ireland, and Kenya. Our founder, Festus Kiprotich, established the company in 2023 with the aim of providing exceptional landscaping services that transform outdoor spaces into beautiful and functional areas. At GiftedHands , we understand that a well-maintained landscape can enhance the curb appeal and value of your property. We are committed to providing comprehensive and personalized landscaping solutions that cater to the unique needs of our clients. Our team of experienced landscapers is skilled in a range of landscaping services, including design, installation, maintenance, and renovation. Our Services Design: We offer professional landscape design services that are tailored to your preferences and needs. Our team will work with you to create a customized design that meets your specific requirements and complements your property's architecture. Installation: Our installation services are second to none. We use the highest quality materials and equipment to ensure that your landscape installation is done right the first time. From irrigation systems to hardscaping, we have the expertise to bring your dream landscape to life. Maintenance: At KullanScapers, we believe that regular maintenance is crucial to keep your landscape looking beautiful and healthy. We offer comprehensive maintenance services, including lawn care, pruning, fertilization, and pest control, among others. Renovation: If your existing landscape needs a makeover, our team can help. We offer renovation services that involve restoring and rejuvenating your landscape to its former glory. Whether you need to upgrade your irrigation system, install new lighting, or add new plants, we have got you covered. Our Mission At GiftedHands Landscapers, our mission is to provide high-quality, sustainable, and affordable landscaping solutions to our clients. We are committed to exceeding our clients' expectations by delivering exceptional services that transform their outdoor spaces into functional, beautiful, and sustainable environments. We pride ourselves on our attention to detail, professionalism, and exceptional customer service. Our Values At GiftedHands Landscapers, we are guided by the following values: Integrity: We believe in honesty, transparency, and accountability in all our dealings. Quality: We are committed to delivering high-quality services that meet and exceed our clients' expectations. Sustainability: We promote sustainable practices in all our landscaping services to protect the environment. Innovation: We embrace innovation and technology to deliver exceptional and efficient landscaping solutions. Community: We are committed to giving back to the communities in which we operate by supporting local initiatives and charities. Contact Us Thank you for considering GiftedHands Landscapers for your landscaping needs. Please feel free to contact us for more information on our services, or to request a consultation. We look forward to hearing from you!

perennial rye-grass (Lolium perenne)

Perennial ryegrass is native to southern Europe, the Middle East, North Africa and eastwards to central Asia. As a useful species of grass for fodder and grazing livestock, it has been taken by farmers settling in new areas including North America, South Africa and Australia. It can be used to prevent erosion and to stabilise soils, as well as creating a hardwearing turf for lawns and golf courses. With its great ability to set seed, its ease of germination and vigour, it has spread from the fields where it has been planted to roadsides, trackways, footpaths, wasteland, river banks and sand dunes. In countries where it has been introduced, it may be regarded as an invasive species that competes with native plants
